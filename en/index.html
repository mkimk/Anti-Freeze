    Anti-Freeze

    《Anti-Freeze》 is the 3rd introduction exhibition for rising artists following HAPJUNGJIGU’s last planning exhibition of 《'zol'jeon》 and 《One Night Before》.Throughout last two times of planning exhibitions the introduction exhibition for rising artists, we felt keenly, if it stayed at the way of consumption of the physical space or commodities, the long-termed prospect couldn`t be desired. We would introduce the rising artists by bringing in the ‘web’ into a stage space through this exhibition set off from this sort of agony. The endeavor trying to utilize the imaginary space might be not a very new one and might also face another limit. Throughout this exhibition we would hereby acknowledge trial and error/ limit and would rather meet the other possibility in basics of webs as ‘sharing fields’ than show off the magnisonant technology.

    The title of 《Anti-Freeze》 are compound words which imply the attitude of display orientation. That is to say, it might not burst into flames or go down heavily but mean loose but incessant movement. The attitude which exhibition claims to advocate is deeply connected by the situation of current rising artists. As the times change, a matter of course becomes not a matter of course further.(it might be a system or way or tradition) The new possibility and opportunity are going to spread. Although it seems to become a era everything possible, it often runs into the presence of still rock-ribbed meta-discourse. In this era, the crisis in which ‘art’ stops working comes in flocks.

    Nonetheless someone often moves by the way of 'art' and make his/her own method supporting the burdensome reality. Exhibition notes how the generation artists called ‘rising’ or ‘young’ react the aspect of today experienced with entire body and which attitude they are facing. They may make disclosure of the aspect in the raw exposed by change or revive the things hidden in the other side or visualize the coming of the new era by positively utilizing the new method the changes bring. The various way they keep could be a struggling for survival going through the desolate situation and a step of development towards wider point. 《Anti-Freeze》 is supporting and orienting the movement not to freeze and herewith expecting all these attempts to connect into the opportunity to expand their basics.
